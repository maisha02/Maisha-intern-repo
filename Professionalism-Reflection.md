# Professionalism at Focus Bear (#10)


## Reflection

## Have you ever experienced or witnessed great teamwork and professionalism? What made it effective?
I experienced strong teamwork during my capstone project last semester. It worked well because of clear and consistent communication. We held regular meetings, shared updates, and supported each other when facing challenges.

## What steps can you take to make sure your communication is clear, respectful, and inclusive?
I will ensure to use simple and clear language, listen carefully, and respond respectfully. I will also make sure everyone feels comfortable asking questions or sharing ideas.

## How can you help create a positive, fun, and professional work environment?
Maintaining a positive mindset and enjoying the learning process helps create a good environment. When I stay engaged and open to learning, it naturally supports a more positive team culture.

## How can you prepare effectively for meetings with supervisors and colleagues?
I can prepare by writing down key points and questions before the meeting. This helps me stay organized and ensures I don’t forget important topics.

## What would it look like to be proactive during your internship?
Being proactive means asking questions, staying curious, and taking initiative. It also means trying to understand tasks deeply rather than just completing them.

## How might you follow up with colleagues or supervisors if you need information/action from them? How would you escalate it further if you don't get a response and when would you escalate?
I would follow up politely through messages or email after some time. If there is still no response and the task is urgent, I would escalate by reaching out again or informing another relevant team member.

## What are some things you won't do?
I don't want to take feedback negatively. Instead, I will treat this as an opportunity to improve. I will also stay respectful and avoid negative communication.
