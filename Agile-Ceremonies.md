# Agile Ceremonies

## Daily Stand-ups
Daily stand-ups are short meetings where team members share what they did, what they will do next, and any blockers. They help keep everyone aligned and aware of progress.

## Sprint Planning vs Continuous Prioritization
Sprint planning is used in Scrum to plan work for a fixed sprint. In Kanban, tasks are prioritized continuously based on current needs and workflow.

## Retrospectives
Retrospectives allow the team to reflect on what went well and what can improve. They help teams learn and improve their process over time.

## Backlog Refinement
Backlog refinement involves reviewing and updating tasks in the backlog. It ensures tasks are clear, prioritized, and ready to be worked on.

-------

## Asynchronous Collaboration
Agile teams use tools like GitHub, Slack, and comments to communicate across time zones. This allows team members to stay updated without needing to be online at the same time.
