# Occupational Health & Safety (OHS) for Desk-Based Work (#12)

# Reflection

## What equipment changes can you make to improve your workspace setup?
My workspace at home includes an external monitor, an adjustable chair, and a monitor stand. This setup helps improve my posture and makes it more comfortable to work for longer periods.

## What behavioural changes can you implement to improve posture and reduce strain?
I can take regular breaks, especially when I start to feel discomfort, and do light stretching. Sitting upright and being mindful of posture also helps reduce strain.

## How can you remind yourself to maintain good posture and take breaks throughout the day?
I can use reminder apps like Focus Bear to schedule breaks and prompt me to adjust my posture. These reminders help me stay consistent throughout the day.
