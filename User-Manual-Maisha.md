# User Manual for Mahir

## About Me
I am a developer intern who is still learning and improving my skills. I like to understand things properly before starting and prefer clear guidance.

## What I Need to Do My Best Work
As someone with ADHD, staying focused takes a lot of effort. For me, I work best when tasks are clear, structured, and broken into smaller steps.

## Communication Style
I prefer simple and direct communication. Async messages work well for me, but I am open to quick calls when something needs clearer explanation.

## Work Habits
I like to focus on one task at a time and avoid switching too often, the switching makes me less focued. Having clear priorities helps me stay on track.

## Staying Productive
A quiet environment and minimal distractions help me focus. Structured to-do lists and clear goals make a big difference for me.

## Feedback Preferences
I prefer clear and direct feedback so I know exactly what to improve. Specific examples help me understand better.

## Giving Feedback
I try to be respectful and clear when giving feedback. I focus on what can be improved in a helpful way.

## Things to Know About Me
I may take a bit of time to fully understand new tasks, but once I do, I work efficiently. I am always willing to learn and improve.

## How to Work Well With Me
Give clear instructions, set priorities, and allow some time for me to focus deeply. In return I will ensure to ask questions and stay aligned with the team.
