Why do you think Focus Bear was created?
Focus Bear was created to help people who get distracted easily, especially those with ADHD. It supports users to stay focused and building good habits.

What problem is Focus Bear solving?
It helps people stay focused and build habits they want to maintain. It mainly reduces distractions and supports daily routines.

Why do you think this mission is important?
I often procrastinate with my work. An app like this helps people like us to stay accountable and improve their habits step by step.

How does Focus Bear’s work align with your personal values or interests?
I often times fall behind on my goals, even simple ones like sleeping on time. Focus Bear could potentially help me to stay consistent.

Do you personally relate to any of the challenges that Focus Bear aims to solve?
Yes, as I struggle with procrastination and staying consistent with building good habits.
