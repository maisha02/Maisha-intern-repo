# Reflection for Issue (#11)

## What steps can you take to ensure you handle data securely in your daily tasks?
I can ensure data security by using strong passwords, enabling 2FA, and avoiding sharing sensitive information. I will also make sure to log out of accounts and keep my devices updated.

## How should you store, share, and dispose of sensitive information safely?
Sensitive information should be stored securely using trusted tools and not kept in plain text. It should only be shared with authorized people and deleted properly if they no longer needed.

## What are some common mistakes that lead to data privacy issues, and how can they be avoided?
Some common mistakes are using weak passwords, clicking suspicious links, and sharing information without verifying. These can be avoided if I am being cautious, verifying sources, and following security best practices.
